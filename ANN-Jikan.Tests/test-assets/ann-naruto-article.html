Tata Play Launches 'Anime Local' Service in Hindi, Tamil, Malayalam

posted on 2024-06-14 10:02 EDT by Adriana Hazra
Launch titles include Naruto, Sergeant Keroro, Ninjaboy Rantaro, Naruto Shippūden, Black Clover, Robotan

The Tata Play (formerly Tata Sky) television service launched its first platform service for anime content on Tuesday. The "Anime Local" service is available in Hindi, Tamil, and Malayalam.

Some notable launch titles for the service include:

  • Naruto
  • Sergeant Keroro
  • Ninjaboy Rantaro (Nintama Rantaro)
  • Naruto Shippūden
  • Black Clover
  • Robotan

Tata Play is launching the add-on service with Sony Yay and Culver Max Entertainment.

The Tata Sky Binge content curation service added KC Global Media's Animax as an OTT (over-the-top) platform in India on March 4. The service launched in June 2021.
